Education

Interaction Design

In this year, the specialty focuses on interaction design, which focuses on creating intuitive and effective user interfaces. Students will learn user-centered design principles, user research methods, rapid prototyping, usability testing, and visual design techniques. In addition, they will acquire skills in popular interaction design tools, such as Adobe XD, Sketch or Figma.

Information Architecture

During this year, students will specialize in information architecture, which refers to the organization and structure of information in software systems. You will learn how to create navigation schematics, taxonomies, site maps, and information flow diagrams. Advanced interaction design techniques such as content-driven design and user experience personalization will also be explored.

User-Centered Design

In this year, the specialty will focus on user-centered design as the main focus. Students will learn to apply more advanced user research techniques such as in-depth interviews, remote usability testing, and task analysis. You will also delve into user-centered design theory, including concepts such as cognitive ergonomics, emotional usability, and inclusive design.
